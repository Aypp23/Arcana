## üí° Pro Tips

- **Be specific:** "ETH price" works, but "What's the 24h change for ETH?" gives more detail
- **Use wallet addresses:** Paste any 0x address for instant analysis
- **Chain names work:** "DEX volume on Arbitrum" or "Yields on Base"
- **Protocol names work:** "Aave TVL" or "Uniswap fees"
- **NFT slugs work:** "Pudgy Penguins floor" or just "Pudgy"
